# Product Management on Soraxi

Effectively managing your product catalog is key to running a successful store on Soraxi. This comprehensive guide will walk you through all aspects of product management, from adding your first product to optimizing existing listings.

## Accessing Product Management

You can access your product management dashboard in two ways:

### Method 1: From Store Sidebar

1. Navigate to your **Store Dashboard**
2. In the left sidebar, find the **"Store Management"** section
3. Click on **"Products"** to access your product catalog

### Method 2: From Store Dashboard

1. Go to your **Store Dashboard**
2. Look for the **"Products"** card in the Quick Actions grid
3. Click **"View Products"** to access your product catalog

## Product Management Dashboard Overview

When you first access the product management page, you'll see:

### Dashboard Header

- **Store name and breadcrumb navigation**
- **"Add Product" button** - Quick access to create new listings
- **Product statistics** - Total count, approved, pending, and hidden products

### Statistics Overview

The dashboard displays four key metrics:

- **Total Products**: All products in your catalog
- **Approved**: Products that have passed verification and are live
- **Pending Review**: Products awaiting Soraxi approval
- **Hidden**: Products you've manually hidden from customers

### Filtering and Search

Easily find specific products using:

- **Search bar**: Search by product name
- **Status filter**: Filter by approval status (All, Approved, Pending, Rejected)
- **Visibility filter**: Show visible, hidden, or all products
- **Apply Filters button**: Refresh results with current filters

## Adding New Products

### Step 1: Access Product Upload Form

Click the **"Add Product"** button from any product management page to start creating a new listing.

### Step 2: Fill Product Information

#### Basic Product Details

- **Product Name** (Required): Clear, descriptive title (max 100 characters)
- **Product Description** (Required): Detailed description using rich text editor
- **Product Specifications** (Optional): Technical details, sizes, materials, etc.

<Tip>
  **Best Practices for Product Titles:**

- Include brand name + product type + key features
- Keep under 60 characters for optimal display
- Use relevant keywords customers might search for
- Example: "Nike Air Max 270 - Men's Running Shoes - Size 10"
  </Tip>

#### Pricing & Inventory

- **Price (₦)** (Required): Set your selling price in Naira
- **Quantity** (Required): Available stock count

<Warning>
  **Pricing Strategy Tips:** - Research competitor pricing on Soraxi - Factor in
  Soraxi commission (8-15%) - Include shipping costs in your calculations -
  Consider psychological pricing (e.g., ₦4,999 vs ₦5,000)
</Warning>

#### Category Selection

- **Main Category** (Required): Select the primary category
- **Subcategory** (Required): Choose the most specific subcategory available

<Screenshot
  src="/docs-images/storefront/soraxi-storefront-product-management-step-5.png"
  alt="Category selection"
  caption="Select the most relevant category and subcategory for your product"
/>

#### Product Images

- Upload up to **6 high-quality images**
- Supported formats: JPEG, PNG, WebP
- Maximum file size: 5MB per image
- **Drag and drop** or click to upload

**Image Requirements:**

- First image is your main product image
- Show product from multiple angles
- Include close-ups of important features
- Use natural lighting when possible
- Show product in use (lifestyle shots)

<Screenshot
  src="/docs-images/storefront/soraxi-storefront-product-management-step-6.png"
  alt="Image upload interface"
  caption="Drag and drop or click to upload product images"
/>

#### Security Verification

- **Store Password** (Required): Enter your store account password for security

### Step 3: Save or Publish

#### Save as Draft

- Use when you're not ready to submit for approval
- Product won't be visible to customers
- You can continue editing later
- Perfect for incomplete listings or seasonal products

#### Publish for Review

- Submits product for Soraxi verification
- Typically reviewed within 24-48 hours
- You'll be notified of approval or rejection
- Once approved, product becomes visible to customers

### Quick Actions Menu

Click the **"⋮"** (three dots) menu for each product to access:

- **View Product**: See how customers see your listing
- **Edit Product**: Modify product details and images
- **Show/Hide Product**: Toggle visibility without deleting

## Editing Products

### Accessing Edit Mode

1. Find the product in your products table
2. Click the **"⋮"** action menu
3. Select **"Edit Product"**
4. You'll be taken to the product edit form

### What You Can Edit

- **All product information** (name, description, specifications)
- **Pricing and inventory** (price, quantity)
- **Categories** (main category and subcategory)
- **Product images** (add, remove, or reorder)
- **Visibility status**

<Warning>
  **Important:** If you edit an approved product, it may require
  re-verification. Major changes could temporarily remove the product from
  search results during review.
</Warning>

## Managing Product Visibility

### Show/Hide Products

You can temporarily hide products without deleting them:

1. Find the product in your table
2. Use the **visibility toggle switch** in the table
3. Or use the **"Show/Hide"** option in the action menu

**When to hide products:**

- Temporarily out of stock
- Seasonal products out of season
- Testing different product variations
- Preparing for product updates

### Bulk Visibility Management

Currently, visibility must be managed per product. For bulk actions, contact store support.

## Product Verification Process

### Understanding Verification

All products go through a verification process to ensure:

- **Accuracy** of product information
- **Appropriateness** for the Soraxi marketplace
- **Compliance** with platform policies
- **Quality** of images and descriptions

### Typical Timeline

- **Draft**: No timeline (you control when to submit)
- **Pending**: 24-48 hours for initial review
- **Approved**: Immediately visible upon approval
- **Rejected**: You'll receive specific feedback for corrections

### Common Rejection Reasons

- **Blurry or low-quality images**
- **Insufficient product information**
- **Inappropriate or prohibited items**
- **Inaccurate category selection**
- **Pricing significantly above market average**

## Best Practices for Product Management

### Optimizing Product Listings

**1. High-Quality Images**

- Use multiple angles (front, back, sides, close-ups)
- Include lifestyle shots showing product in use
- Ensure good lighting and clear focus
- Use white backgrounds for main images

**2. Compelling Descriptions**

- Start with key benefits and features
- Use bullet points for easy scanning
- Include sizing information and specifications
- Mention shipping and return policies

**3. Strategic Pricing**

- Research competitor pricing
- Consider bundle pricing for related items
- Offer occasional promotions
- Factor in all costs (production, shipping, fees)

### Inventory Management Tips

**1. Stock Level Alerts**

- Regularly update stock quantities
- Set minimum stock alerts for yourself
- Plan for restocking lead times
- Consider seasonal demand fluctuations

**2. Product Organization**

- Use consistent naming conventions
- Group related products with similar categories
- Regularly review and update product information
- Archive discontinued products

## Troubleshooting Common Issues

### "My Product is Stuck in Pending"

**Solution:**

- Check your email for verification status updates
- Ensure all required fields are completed
- Verify images meet quality requirements
- Contact support if pending for more than 48 hours

### "Product Was Rejected"

**Next Steps:**

1. Check email for specific rejection reasons
2. Make necessary corrections
3. Resubmit for review
4. Contact support if you need clarification

### "Can't Upload Images"

**Common Issues:**

- File size too large (max 5MB per image)
- Wrong file format (use JPEG, PNG, or WebP)
- Too many images (max 6 per product)
- Poor internet connection

### "Price/Quantity Changes Not Saving"

**Check:**

- You have edit permissions for the product
- You're using valid number formats
- Your store account is in good standing
- You've clicked "Save" after making changes

## Frequently Asked Questions

**Q: How many products can I list?**
A: There's no limit! You can list as many products as you want.

**Q: Can I transfer products between stores?**
A: Currently, products are tied to specific stores. You'd need to recreate listings for other stores.

**Q: How long do products stay in draft mode?**
A: Draft products remain indefinitely until you publish or delete them.

**Q: Can I schedule product releases?**
A: Not currently, but you can use the hide/show feature to control visibility.

**Q: What happens if I run out of stock?**
A: Set quantity to 0 or hide the product until restocked to prevent orders.

**Q: Can I sell services, not just products?**
A: Support for selling services will be available soon. Please stay tuned for updates..
