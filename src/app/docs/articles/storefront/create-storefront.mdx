# How to Create a Storefront on Soraxi

Welcome to Soraxi! This comprehensive guide will walk you through the entire process of creating your own storefront. Whether you're a student entrepreneur, small business owner, or individual seller, we've made the process simple and straightforward.

## What You'll Need

Before you start, make sure you have:

- A verified Soraxi account
- Your business information ready
- Store name and branding ideas
- Product information (optional for initial setup)
- Bank account details for payouts (can be added later)

<Note type="info">
  Creating a storefront is completely free! Soraxi only charges a small
  commission on successful sales, not upfront fees.
</Note>

## Step-by-Step Store Creation Guide

### Step 1: Access Your Profile

1. **Locate your username** at the top right-hand side of any Soraxi page
2. **Click on your username** to open the dropdown menu
3. **Select "Profile"** from the menu options

<Screenshot
  src="/docs-images/account/soraxi-verification-step-1.png"
  alt="User profile dropdown menu"
  caption="Click your username and select 'Profile' from the dropdown"
/>

<Warning>
  Your account must be verified before you can create a storefront. If you see a
  verification prompt, complete that first.
</Warning>

### Step 2: Start Store Creation

1. On your profile page, scroll to the **"My Stores"** section
2. If you have no existing stores, you'll see an empty state with a **"Create Your First Store"** button
3. Click the button to begin the store creation process

<Screenshot
  src="/docs-images/storefront/soraxi-storefront-step-1.png"
  alt="My Stores section on profile page"
  caption="Click 'Create Your First Store' in the My Stores section"
/>

<Tip>
  If you already have stores, click the "Add Store" button in the My Stores
  section header to create additional storefronts.
</Tip>

### Step 3: Store Creation Landing Page

You'll be redirected to the store onboarding landing page that highlights:

- **Key benefits** of selling on Soraxi
- **Success statistics** and seller testimonials
- **Feature comparisons** with social media selling
- **Simple 3-step process** overview

<Screenshot
  src="/docs-images/storefront/soraxi-storefront-step-2.png"
  alt="Store onboarding landing page"
  caption="Learn about the benefits of selling on Soraxi before creating your store"
/>

Click **"Create Your Store Now"** to proceed to the store creation form.

### Step 4: Create Store Account

Fill out the store creation form with:

1. **Store Name** (required): Your brand or business name
2. **Store Email** (required): Dedicated email for store communications
3. **Password** (required): Secure password for store account
4. **Confirm Password** (required): Re-enter your password

<Screenshot
  src="/docs-images/storefront/soraxi-storefront-step-3.png"
  alt="Store creation form"
  caption="Enter your store's basic information to create the account"
/>

<Warning>
  Your store name and email must be unique across the platform. If you get an
  error, try a different name or email.
</Warning>

### Step 5: Store Profile Setup (Onboarding Step 1)

After successful store creation, you'll begin the onboarding process:

1. **Store Name**: Already provided during sign-up
2. **Store Description**: Write a compelling description (150-500 characters)

**Recommended store description template:**
_"We provide high-quality [products/services] with fast shipping and excellent customer service. Thank you for supporting our small business!"_

### Step 6: Business Information (Onboarding Step 2)

Provide your business details for verification:

1. **Business Type**: Individual (Partnership coming soon)

<Tip>
  Individual sellers can use their personal information if they don't have a
  registered business.
</Tip>

<Screenshot
  src="/docs-images/storefront/soraxi-storefront-step-5.png"
  alt="Business information form"
  caption="Step 2: Provide your business details for verification"

### Step 7: Shipping Configuration (Onboarding Step 3)

Set up how you'll deliver products to customers:

1. **Shipping Method Setup**:
   - **Method Name**: Give your shipping option a clear name (e.g., "Standard Delivery", "Express Shipping")
   - **Shipping Price**: Set the cost in Naira (₦) for this delivery method
   - **Estimated Delivery Days**: Specify how many days it typically takes to deliver

2. **Method Description** (Optional):
   - Add clear details about the shipping service
   - Include any special instructions or conditions
   - Example: "Regular delivery within 3-5 business days"

<Tip>
  **Recommended Settings for New Sellers:** - **Name**: "Standard Delivery" -
  **Price**: ₦500 - ₦1,500 (depending on your product size) - **Delivery Time**:
  3-5 days to set realistic expectations - **Description**: "We deliver within
  3-5 business days."
</Tip>

**After completing this step**: Click "Continue to Terms" to proceed to the final step of store setup.

Review and agree to Soraxi's terms:

1. **Seller Agreement (Terms of Service)**: Platform rules and responsibilities
2. **Fee Structure**: Commission rates and payment terms
3. **Privacy Policy**: Data handling and customer information
4. **Code of Conduct**: Marketplace behavior expectations

## After Onboarding Completion

Once you complete all onboarding steps:

1. **Store Dashboard Access**: You'll be redirected to your store dashboard
2. **Pending Verification**: Your store will be reviewed (typically 24-48 hours)
3. **Add Products**: Start listing your products immediately
4. **Store Status**: Track your verification progress in "Store Profile"

## Store Status Explained

Your store will go through these statuses:

- **Pending**: Under review by Soraxi team
- **Active**: Approved and ready to sell
- **Rejected**: Needs corrections (you'll receive feedback)
- **Suspended**: Temporarily inactive (contact support)

## Adding Your First Products

Once your store is created (and approved), you can:

1. Go to **Products** → **Add New Product**
2. Fill in product details:
   - Product name and description
   - Price and inventory quantity
   - High-quality images (minimum 3 recommended)
   - Category and tags
3. Set variants if needed (sizes, colors, etc.)
4. Click **Save as Draft** or **Publish**

<Tip>
  Add at least 10-15 products before your store goes live. This builds
  credibility and gives customers more choices.
</Tip>

## Verification Checklist

Before your storefront can go active, ensure you've completed:

- ✓ All onboarding steps (Profile, Business, Shipping, Terms)
- ✓ Store profile information complete
- ✓ At least one product listed
- ✓ Business information verified (if applicable)
- ✓ Shipping settings configured

## Common Issues & Troubleshooting

### "Store name already exists"

**Solution**:

- Try adding your location or specialty to the name
- Use abbreviations or different spelling
- Check if you already have a store with that name

### "Verification is taking too long"

**Typical timeline**:

- Initial review: 24-48 hours
- Complex businesses: 3-5 business days
- If longer: Contact support with your store ID

### "I can't complete onboarding"

**Common fixes**:

- Ensure all required fields are filled
- Check internet connection stability
- Clear browser cache and cookies
- Try using a different browser

### "My store was rejected"

**Next steps**:

1. Check your email for specific rejection reasons
2. Make the required corrections
3. Contact support if you need clarification
4. Resubmit for review

## What's Next After Store Creation?

After your store is active:

1. **Optimize Your Listings** - Add detailed descriptions and multiple images
2. **Build Your Reputation** - Provide excellent service to earn reviews
3. **Expand Your Inventory** - Regularly add new products

## Store Management Tips

### Best Practices for New Sellers

- **Respond quickly** to new orders from customers (under 2 hours)
- **Update inventory regularly** to avoid overselling
- **Use high-quality photos** - they significantly impact sales
- **Offer multiple shipping options** - cater to different customer needs

### Pricing Strategy

- Research similar products on Soraxi
- Factor in Soraxi commission
- Include shipping costs in your pricing calculations
- Consider offering bundle deals to increase average order value

## FAQs

**Q: Can I change my store name later?**
A: Yes, but frequent changes may confuse customers. Contact support for major name changes.

**Q: How many stores can I create?**
A: You can create multiple stores for different product categories or brands.

**Q: Is there a monthly fee?**
A: No! Soraxi is free to use. We only take a commission on sales.

**Q: How do I get paid?**
A: Payouts are processed weekly. Set up your bank account in Store Settings → Payment Setup.

**Q: What if I want to pause selling temporarily?**
A: You can deactivate your store in settings. Your products won't be visible, but you can reactivate later.

**Q: Can I sell services, not just products?**
A: Support for selling services will be available soon. Please stay tuned for updates..
