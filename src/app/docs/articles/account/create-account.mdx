# How to Create Your Soraxi Account

Welcome to Soraxi! Creating your account is the first step toward accessing our platform's features. This guide will walk you through the simple sign-up process.

## What You'll Need

Before you start, make sure you have:

- A valid email address
- A strong password (meets our security requirements)
- Your personal information ready
- Your address details

<Note type="info">Creating a Soraxi account is completely free!</Note>

## Step-by-Step Account Creation

### Step 1: Access the Sign-Up Page

1. Go to [www.soraxihub.com/sign-up](https://www.soraxihub.com/sign-up)
2. You'll see the Soraxi welcome page with the sign-up form

### Step 2: Personal Details

The first step collects your basic personal information:

1. **First Name** (required): Your legal first name
2. **Last Name** (required): Your legal last name
3. **Other Names** (optional): Any middle names or additional names

<Tip>
  Use your legal name as it appears on official documents. This helps with
  future verification if you decide to become a vendor.
</Tip>

### Step 3: Address Information

The second step collects your location details:

1. **Street Address** (required): Your complete street address
2. **City** (required): Your city of residence
3. **State** (required): Your state or province
4. **Postal Code** (required): Your ZIP or postal code

<Warning>
  Ensure your address is accurate as it will be used for shipping and account
  verification purposes.
</Warning>

### Step 4: Contact & Security

The final step sets up your login credentials:

1. **Email Address** (required): Your primary email for account communication
2. **Phone Number** (required): Your contact phone number
3. **Password** (required): Create a secure password
4. **Confirm Password** (required): Re-enter your password

## Password Requirements

Soraxi takes security seriously. Your password must meet these criteria:

- ✓ At least 8 characters long
- ✓ Contains uppercase letters (A-Z)
- ✓ Contains lowercase letters (a-z)
- ✓ Includes numbers (0-9)
- ✓ Includes special characters (!@#$%^&\*)

The form provides real-time feedback on your password strength:

- **Weak** (red): Meets few requirements
- **Moderate** (yellow): Meets most requirements
- **Strong** (green): Meets all requirements

## Navigation Between Steps

- Click **"Next"** to proceed to the next step
- Click **"Previous"** to go back to earlier steps
- The progress indicator shows which step you're on

<Tip>
  You can navigate back to previous steps if you need to correct information.
  Your data is saved as you progress.
</Tip>

## Finalizing Your Account

1. Review all entered information on the final step
2. Click **"Sign Up"** to create your account
3. You'll see a loading indicator while your account is being created

## What Happens Next?

After successful sign-up:

1. **First Login**: You'll be redirected to the sign-in page. Sign in with your new credentials
2. **Welcome Email**: You'll receive a Welcome email from Soraxi

## Common Issues & Troubleshooting

### "I'm getting validation errors"

**Common solutions**:

- Ensure all required fields are filled
- Check email format (must be valid email address)
- Verify passwords match exactly
- Ensure phone number is in correct format

### "The form won't let me proceed to next step"

**Check these**:

- All current step fields are completed correctly
- No validation errors (red text under fields)
- Password meets all strength requirements

## Account Security Tips

<Warning>
  Never share your password with anyone. Soraxi staff will never ask for your
  password.
</Warning>

- Use a unique password not used on other sites
- Keep your email account secure
- Log out from shared devices

## FAQs

**Q: Can I change my information after signing up?**
A: Yes! You can update most information in your account settings after verification.

**Q: Can I use the same email for multiple accounts?**
A: No, each email address can only be associated with one Soraxi account.

**Q: What if I forget my password?**
A: Use the "Forgot Password" feature on the sign-in page to reset it.
